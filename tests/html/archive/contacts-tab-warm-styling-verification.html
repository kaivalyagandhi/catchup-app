Visual Inspection Guide

What to look for:

  • Warm backgrounds: Cards should have a warm alabaster/cream feel in Latte mode, not stark white
  • Warm borders: Borders should be warm gray (Stone), not cool blue-gray
  • Text hierarchy: Primary text (names) should be deep stone, secondary text (email/phone) should be lighter stone
  • Avatar colors: Should feel earthy and organic (sage green, sandy beige, dusty rose, warm stone)
  • Tag badges: Warm blue tint, not stark blue
  • Group badges: Warm amber/terracotta tones, not bright green
  • Hover states: Should subtly highlight with warm tones
  • Dark mode: Should maintain warmth with deep coffee backgrounds, not pure black
