# CatchUp Product Vision

CatchUp is an AI-powered relationship management application that reduces coordination friction and helps users maintain meaningful connections across relationship tiers.

## Core Goals

- Identify which friends to reach out to and when
- Track time since last meaningful interaction across relationship tiers
- Facilitate connection through intelligent scheduling and suggestions
- Reduce coordination overhead for maintaining friendships

## Key Principles

- Productivity-focused: Prioritize keeping in touch efficiently
- Reduce coordination friction as primary goal
- User control: AI suggests, user decides
- Flexible across relationship depths: Close friends to broader network
- Context-aware: Calendar, interests, time since last contact, individual preferences

## User Personas

- **Active Connectors**: 5-15 close friends, regular contact
- **Relationship Organizers**: ~25 good friends, consistent contact
- **Network Maintainers**: 50+ acquaintances, periodic connection

## Core Value Proposition

Transform passive relationship maintenance into active, intelligent connection opportunities by combining calendar awareness, interest matching, and time-based triggers with individual frequency preferences.
